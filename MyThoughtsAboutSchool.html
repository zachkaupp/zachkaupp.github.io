School

There are some things I like about school and some I do not. I'll make some lists.

Things I like

  • The idea of learning! I want to know stuff
  • The opportunity to get a good job and have a future
  • The ability to meet new people
  • The ability to try new things

Things I don't like

  • When I get a bad grade because of things that don't make sense (Like writing an essay about science in LA)
  • Bad friends
  • Classes that don't have a good structure
  • Bad teachers
  • Teachers that base their class off of and online course (Especially free/multiple ones)
